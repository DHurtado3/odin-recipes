Bowl of Cereal

The Go-To Savior: The Bowl of Cereal

One of the ultimate simple snacks you can have at any time and it will always taste great. The bowl of cereal can be enjoyed by anyone at any age. There is only 2 ingrediants in this simple snack.

Ingrediants

  • Cereal of your choice
  • Milk of your choice

Steps

  1. Add cereal to your bowl. How much is dependant upon how much cereal you want to eat.
  2. Add milk to your bowl. How much is dependant upon much milk you would like.
