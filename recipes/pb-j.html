Peanut Butter & Jelly Sandwich

If you're ever feeling snacky and want something quick and easy to make, you can never go wrong with the classic Peanut Butter and Jelly sandwich. You just need 4 ingrediants and you'll be ready to go.

Ingrediants

  • 2 slices of Bread
  • Peanut Butter
  • Jelly

Steps

  1. On one slice of bread, add a tablespoon of peanut butter and spread
  2. On the other slice, add a tablespoon of jelly
  3. Add one piece of bread on top of the other so that the peanut butter and jelly touch

It is optional to have with a nice cold glass of milk to add on flavor
